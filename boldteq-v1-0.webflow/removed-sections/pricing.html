Frequently Asked Questions

See our Help Center or send us a message!

What exactly does Boldteq do?

Boldteq becomes the delivery team behind your agency.
We provide a fully managed design & development backend — including project ownership, QA review, and live request tracking — so you can scale client work without hiring or managing in-house teams.

Is Boldteq an agency, a tool, or a staffing service?

Neither.
Boldteq is a delivery infrastructure.
‍You get a dedicated execution team + a delivery system to submit requests, track progress, and manage feedback — all under one subscription.
No freelancers. No staff leasing. No chaotic outsourcing.

Who is Boldteq built for?

Boldteq is built for digital agencies that:
• want predictable delivery
• need reliable execution partners
• are tired of hiring delays or freelancer churn
• handle ongoing client work
• care about quality and accountability
Whether you’re early-stage or high-volume, Boldteq adapts to your workflow.

What happens after we subscribe?

You’ll get:
• onboarding with your delivery owner
• access to your private delivery dashboard
• workflow alignment
• brand standard setup
Then you start submitting requests.
Delivery begins immediately.

How do we submit and manage work?

You submit tasks through your private Boldteq delivery dashboard.
From there you can:
• create requests
• track status in real time
• communicate directly on each task
• give feedback
• approve deliveries
Everything stays organized in one place.

Who is responsible for quality and final delivery?

Boldteq alway takes the ownership for quality and delivery.
Every account has a dedicated delivery owner, and every task goes through internal QA before reaching you.
You’re never left chasing developers or fixing broken work.

Can Boldteq handle large or ongoing projects?

Yes.Boldteq is built for continuous delivery — whether you’re running multiple client projects or long-term builds.
High-volume agencies use Boldteq as their permanent backend.

Is Boldteq suitable for both early-stage and high-volume agencies?

Yes.Early agencies use Boldteq to avoid hiring too early.
Scaling agencies use Boldteq to increase capacity without operational chaos.
